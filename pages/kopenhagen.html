CMS - Kopenhagen

For this school assignment we had to redesign the website of a Danish website about art in Copenhagen and the southern Sweden. We had to sort all their content into different categories using wordpress as a backend. When we coded the page, we used JavaScript to filter the categories. It was a lot of hard work, but it paid off as the owner of Kopenhagen really liked our visions.

The website isn’t fully working, but you can get an idea of the project on the website.
