About me

My name is Astrid and I am studying multimedia design student at KEA, Copenhagen school of design and technology, currently on 3rd semester. As a multimedia designer I have worked with different levels of digital design process and I have gained different skills within design and programming. I have worked with style guides, visual identity, branding, UX, UI, HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I am comfortable using GitHub for programming collaboration and as an online saving tool. I have a great understanding of how to work a good part of the Adobe programs.


When I’m not spending time making school projects, I love to hang out in my Kolonihave in Skovlunde. It is my sanctuary where I can get my hands dirty when I am doing gardening in my utility garden. It is also where I can open my mind for inspiration and creativity. When I am at home in Copenhagen I love to bike around town on my city-bike and visit all my friends. When I am at home chilling, I try to expand my crocheting-skills while watching the latest TV-series.
